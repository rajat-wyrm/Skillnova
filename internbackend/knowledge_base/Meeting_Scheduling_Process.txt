Meeting Scheduling Process

Effective meetings are essential for team alignment, progress tracking, and mentorship during your internship.

1. Scheduling a Meeting
- Navigate to "Meetings" from the sidebar menu.
- Click "Schedule New Meeting" and fill in the title, date, time, and participants.
- Choose between "One-on-One" (mentor meetings) or "Team" (group standups).
- All participants will receive an email notification and calendar invite.

2. Meeting Types
- Daily Standup: Brief 15-minute check-in with your team. Share what you did yesterday, what you plan to do today, and any blockers.
- Weekly Mentor Meeting: 30-minute session with your assigned mentor to discuss progress, get feedback, and plan next steps.
- Sprint Review: End-of-sprint presentation of completed work to stakeholders.
- Knowledge Sharing Session: Optional sessions where interns present topics they've learned.

3. Meeting Etiquette
- Join on time — late arrivals disrupt the flow.
- Come prepared with updates and questions.
- Keep your camera on during video calls.
- Mute yourself when not speaking.
- Take notes and share action items after the meeting.

4. Rescheduling / Cancellation
- If you need to reschedule, update the meeting at least 24 hours in advance.
- Notify all participants through the platform messaging system.
- Repeated no-shows will be flagged in your performance review.
