Attendance Policy

Consistent attendance and active participation are essential for maximizing the value of your internship at UptoSkills.

1. Working Hours
- Standard Hours: Interns are expected to adhere to the agreed-upon standard working hours for their specific role and time zone.
- Flexibility: If your internship offers flexible hours, ensure you overlap with your mentor's schedule for core collaboration periods.

2. Reporting Attendance
- Daily Log-in: Log in to the UptoSkills platform or the designated communication channel at the beginning of your workday.
- Status Updates: Keep your status on communication channels updated (e.g., "Available," "In a Meeting," "Offline") so your team knows your availability.

3. Leave Requests
- Planned Leave: Submit requests for planned leave (e.g., exams, personal commitments) to your mentor and HR at least one week in advance.
- Sick Leave: In case of illness or unforeseen emergencies, notify your mentor and HR as soon as possible before the start of your workday.
- Unplanned Absences: Frequent unplanned absences may negatively impact your internship evaluation.

4. Holidays
- Interns are entitled to observe the national and regional public holidays as outlined in the company calendar. Please confirm your specific holiday schedule with your HR representative.
