Weekly Report Submission Guidelines

All interns are required to submit a weekly progress report documenting their work, learnings, and challenges.

1. Submission Schedule
- Reports must be submitted every Friday before 6:00 PM IST.
- Late submissions will be flagged and may affect your performance rating.
- If you will be absent on Friday, submit your report on Thursday.

2. Report Structure
Your weekly report should include the following sections:
- Summary: Brief overview of what you accomplished this week (2-3 sentences).
- Tasks Completed: List of specific tasks you finished, with descriptions.
- Tasks In Progress: Work that is ongoing and expected completion date.
- Challenges Faced: Any blockers or difficulties encountered.
- Learnings: Key technical or soft skills you developed this week.
- Plan for Next Week: What you intend to work on in the coming week.

3. How to Submit
- Navigate to "My Reports" from the sidebar.
- Click the "New Report" button.
- Fill in all required fields.
- Click "Submit" — you will receive a confirmation notification.
- You can view past reports and their review status in the same section.

4. Review Process
- Your mentor will review your report within 3 business days.
- Reports are scored on a scale of 1-10 based on thoroughness, clarity, and accuracy.
- Constructive feedback will be provided alongside the score.
- You can view your score and feedback in the "My Reports" section.

5. Best Practices
- Be specific: "Implemented user authentication using JWT" is better than "Worked on authentication."
- Include metrics where possible: "Reduced API response time by 40%."
- Be honest about challenges — this shows self-awareness and maturity.
- Proofread your report before submission.
