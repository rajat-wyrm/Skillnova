Practical Implementation Guides for SkillNova Interns

## Weekly Report Submission - Step by Step

1. **Access the Reports Section**
   - Log into your SkillNova dashboard
   - Click "My Reports" in the left sidebar menu
   - Look for the "New Report" button (top right corner)

2. **Fill Your Weekly Report**
   - **Summary**: Write 2-3 sentences about your week's accomplishments
   - **Tasks Completed**: List each task with a brief description of what you delivered
   - **Tasks In Progress**: Mention ongoing work and expected completion dates
   - **Challenges Faced**: Be honest about blockers and how you tried to solve them
   - **Learnings**: Include both technical skills and soft skills you developed
   - **Next Week Plan**: List specific tasks you'll work on

3. **Submit Before Deadline**
   - Deadline: Every Friday before 6:00 PM IST
   - If you'll be absent on Friday, submit on Thursday
   - Double-check all required fields are filled before clicking "Submit"

## Attendance Management - Daily Actions

### Daily Login Process
1. **Morning Login (9:00 AM your time)**
   - Log into SkillNova platform
   - Update your status to "Available"
   - Check for any urgent messages from your mentor

2. **Status Updates During Day**
   - Change status to "In a Meeting" when attending calls
   - Set to "Offline" only when your workday is complete
   - Keep status accurate so your team knows availability

### When You Need to Be Absent
1. **Planned Leave**
   - Submit request at least 1 week in advance
   - Include reason and dates in your request
   - Get approval from both mentor and HR

2. **Emergency Absence**
   - Notify mentor BEFORE start of workday
   - Send message via platform and email
   - Log absence in attendance module the same day

3. **Medical Leave**
   - Notify mentor immediately
   - Submit doctor's note within 3 days
   - Use medical leave option in attendance system

## DSA Practice - How to Use the Platform

1. **Navigate to DSA Section**
   - Go to "Projects" from sidebar
   - Select "DSA Practice" module
   - Choose your difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard

2. **Problem Solving Workflow**
   - Read the problem description carefully
   - Check input/output format examples
   - Write your solution in the provided editor
   - Test with sample cases before submitting
   - Submit and check your results

3. **Learning Resources**
   - Each problem includes related topics
   - Use the "Hint" feature if stuck (reduces points)
   - Review solutions after 3 attempts
   - Track your progress in the analytics section

## Meeting Scheduling - Best Practices

1. **Requesting Meetings with Mentor**
   - Use the "Schedule Meeting" button in mentor's profile
   - Propose 2-3 time slots that work for you
   - Include meeting agenda and required preparation
   - Send request at least 24 hours in advance

2. **Meeting Preparation**
   - Review your weekly progress before the meeting
   - Prepare specific questions about blockers
   - Have your task list ready for discussion
   - Test your video/audio setup 10 minutes before

3. **During the Meeting**
   - Share your screen when discussing code
   - Take notes on action items and deadlines
   - Ask clarifying questions if unsure
   - Confirm next steps before ending

## Task Validation - Getting Your Work Approved

1. **Before Submitting Tasks**
   - Review all requirements in the task description
   - Test your implementation thoroughly
   - Document any assumptions or limitations
   - Prepare a brief summary of your approach

2. **Submission Process**
   - Click "Submit Task" in your dashboard
   - Upload all required files and documentation
   - Include links to live demos if applicable
   - Add comments explaining your solution

3. **After Submission**
   - Monitor feedback in your task history
   - Address reviewer comments promptly
   - Resubmit with requested changes
   - Mark task as complete only after approval

## Common Issues and Quick Solutions

### Can't Submit Weekly Report
- **Issue**: Submit button is disabled
- **Solution**: Check if all required fields are filled
- **Backup**: Contact mentor if deadline is approaching

### Attendance Not Marked
- **Issue**: Daily attendance not showing
- **Solution**: Ensure you clicked "Log Attendance" button
- **Backup**: Email mentor with your login time

### DSA Problems Not Loading
- **Issue**: Practice problems show blank screen
- **Solution**: Clear browser cache and refresh
- **Backup**: Try different browser or contact support

### Meeting Request Not Accepted
- **Issue**: Mentor hasn't responded to meeting request
- **Solution**: Send polite follow-up message after 24 hours
- **Backup**: Try alternative time slots

## Emergency Contacts

- **Technical Support**: support@skillnova.com
- **HR Department**: hr@skillnova.com  
- **Urgent Mentor Issues**: Use emergency contact in mentor profile
- **Platform Issues**: Use the "Report Issue" button in help section

Remember: When in doubt, ask your mentor or use the help section. It's better to ask than to make assumptions!
